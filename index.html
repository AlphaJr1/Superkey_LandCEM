Objectives

  • Prepare individuals to become certified and skilled business mentors
  • Increase knowledge and skills in guiding and mentoring entrepreneurs
  • Build a network and community of professional business mentors

Training Materials

  • Fundamental Business: Basic entrepreneurship concepts, types of businesses, business models, business planning, financial management, business operations
  • Mentoring and Coaching Guide: Principles, techniques, roles and responsibilities, ethics
  • Business Licensing: Types, processes, requirements, tips and tricks
  • Marketing: Strategies, digital marketing, branding, sales, customer relationship management
  • Funding: Understanding funding, sources, needs analysis, short-term and long-term funding, risk management, trends, and innovations
  • Partnerships: Building and maintaining business partnerships, negotiation, and communication
  • Public Speaking: Effective techniques, engaging and persuasive delivery, handling audiences
